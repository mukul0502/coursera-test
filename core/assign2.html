MATHEMATICS

ALPHA

Alpha is often used in physics and chemistry as a symbol or in the names of things, for example alpha particles.The symbol is also used a lot in mathematics in algebra, for example naming angles.

BETA

Beta is the second letter of the Greek alphabet.Beta is transliterated as b in Classical Greek.The Greek letter Beta is especially used in finance, science, mathematics, statistics and typography.

GAMMA

Gamma is the third letter of the Greek alphabet. In the system of Greek numerals it has a value of 3.In Modern Greek, this letter represents either a voiced velar fricative or a voiced palatal fricative.
